FAQs

How does Refill & Go work?

Bring your own containers to our truck, choose your products, and refill as much or as little as you need. Pay contactlessly and help reduce single-use packaging!

Where can I find the truck?

Check our Schedule page for the latest locations and times. You can also track the truck in real-time using our app.

What products do you offer?

We offer a range of household essentials, personal care items, and dry foods. See the Products section for details.

Do I need to download the app?

The app is recommended for real-time truck tracking, digital payments, and exclusive offers, but you can also shop at the truck without it.

Can I request the truck in my neighborhood?

Yes! Use the app or our Contact Us page to suggest a stop.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other contactless payments.
